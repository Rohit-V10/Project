ALL ABOUT ME

Hello, there amigos! My name is Rohit, Welcome to All About Me. Now you would be wondering who am I. If I were to describe myself in 3 words it would be: Optimistic, Ambitious and Gallant. Now why do these 3 adjectives specifically describe me?
Simple, In a world where as a part of human nature when things don't go your way, you feel demotivated and uninspired to achieve your goals. I too as a human, tend to feel them as well but I live by a proverb that frequently gets me back right on my feet, this is "If it[the problem] won't matter over 5 years, it's not worth worrying about for 5 minutes".
Ambitious that's an adjective that normally defines people with a goal and plan. Me I got Goals and I stick through them regardless of the circumstances.
Gallant? Yeah, I am a man of pushing my limits, regardless of retreating out on things I fear, I rather Face Everything And Rise (F.E.A.R) that's what it means to me, to become a new version of me every day.

That's Who I Am in a nutshell.

Strengths

Out of the numerous strengths, I believe I have, I feel that my courage when handling any situation at hand defines who I am the most. For me, being courageous is having the ability to face my fears with a positive, unrelenting attitude.
An example of me displaying this strength is when I joined the student learning community (SLC). I was extremely nervous about taking on this role as it involves speaking in public, but I persevered and even went on to publicly debate against much more experienced students than myself.Although I was initially worried about the event, I strived to succeed, and I did face my fears.
Being courageous has also helped me academically, as I have the confidence to ask questions when I need to.
My strengths are more suited to the ongoing nature of formative assessments, rather than the high stakes set by summative assessments because I can rectify any issues with my studies in a timely fashion. Furthermore, if I gain an understanding of how well I am doing, I immediately feel encouraged to improve.
